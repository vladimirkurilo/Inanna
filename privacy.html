PRIVACY POLICY At Inanna Birthing Center, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the information we may collect from you, how it is used, and the steps we take to ensure that it is kept secure. When you visit our website, we may collect certain information from you, including your name, email address, and other personal information. This information is used to provide you with a better user experience, to respond to any inquiries or requests you may have, and to improve our services. We may also collect information related to the use of our website, such as IP addresses, browsing activity, pages visited, and other technical information. This data is used to analyze and improve the performance of our website and services. We may also collect information from social media platforms, if you choose to follow or engage with us on these sites. This information may include your name, profile picture, and other information you have made public on the platform. We will never share your personal information with any third parties, unless we are required to do so by law or you have given us permission to do so. We take security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access and to ensure that it is only used in accordance with this Privacy Policy. We use SSL encryption technology to protect the transmission of your data. We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or services. We will notify you of any changes by posting the updated Privacy Policy on our website. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
ABOUT US Inanna Birthing Center is a specialized birthing center located in the heart of London. Our mission is to provide a safe, comfortable, and supportive environment for women throughout their pregnancy, childbirth, and postpartum journey. We strive to provide evidence-based care and support through a team of professional and experienced midwives, nurses, and certified practitioners. Our dedicated staff is committed to providing personalized care and support, tailored to each individual’s needs and preferences. At Inanna Birthing Center, we believe in the power of the birthing process, and we provide our clients with the best possible care and experience throughout their journey. We specialize in water births, natural birth, unassisted birth, home birth, VBAC (vaginal birth after cesarean), and postpartum care. Our facility is equipped with state-of-the-art amenities, such as birthing tubs, birthing beds, and private rooms for labor and delivery. At Inanna Birthing Center, we are passionate about helping women achieve the best birth experience possible. We strive to provide a warm, caring, and nurturing atmosphere, where clients can feel safe and supported throughout their journey. We look forward to helping you create a beautiful, meaningful, and positive birthing experience!
