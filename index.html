Our Center

We are dedicated to providing expecting moms and their expanding families with top-notch midwifery care. The Inanna Birthing Center provides you with individualized support and safe, pleasant midwifery care for your entire pregnancy, labor, and postpartum period.

LEarn more

Baby

Your baby will be gently welcomed into the loving arms of an experienced midwife.  Our Center is equipped to safely care for your normal pregnancy.  In the rare cases where complications develop, our team of midwives and physicians will care for you in a nearby hospital where the complications can be safely managed.

Pregn​ancy

Labor and delivery do not have to take place in a medical facility for low-risk pregnancies. For patients who are at minimal risk for giving delivery, our lovely birthing rooms were created to be cozy, give a calm, and safe environment.

Initial Assessment Care

We offer comprehensive and personalized initial assessment to ensure the health and safety of both mother and baby. Our services include routine checkups, nutrition counseling, and health education. 

Newborn Care

We provide comprehensive newborn care for both you and your baby. Our services include newborn screenings, infant care, and immunizations.

Water Birth

A waterbirth is when part of your labor, your delivery or both happen while you are immersed in water. The tubs can accommodate your partner or support person should you so desire.

what we do

At Inanna Birthing Center, we provide a range of services to help expectant mothers have a safe and comfortable birthing experience. These services include Initial Assessment care, labor and delivery care, and lactation support. Our birthing center staff consists of Certified Nurse-Midwife (CNM) and Certified Nurse Practitioner (CNP) who are dedicated to providing care and support to mothers and their families.
